# Design Partner Program

> Acme Notes runs a closed beta with 8 design partners — small teams (5–50 people) who get the product free in exchange for weekly feedback. The program ends at public launch (~2 months out).

## What we offer

- Free use during beta
- Weekly 30-min calls with the founder
- Direct line on issues (Slack channel per partner)
- Influence on roadmap (their requests get priority)

## What we expect

- Active use (3+ team members, 10+ notes/week)
- 30 minutes of feedback per week
- Bug reports with reproduction steps

## Current partners

- **Bluefin Coffee** — 12-person ops team. Daily SOPs use case. Open ask: PDF export with their branding.
- **Tessera Studio** — 8-person design agency. Client briefs use case. Renewal 2026-06-01. Asked about SAML SSO (deferred to Q3).

(6 more partners — fill in via ingest as their context lands.)

## Exit criteria

Partners stay on free beta until public launch. At launch:

- Convert to paid (50% discount for first year), OR
- Graceful exit with full data export
